Hi, my name is Qihong Lu (吕其鸿). I will move to Columbia University as a postdoc fellow this Fall to work on computational models of context and episodic memory!
Here's my CV.

I received my PhD in Cognitive Psychology at Princeton University, where I worked with Ken Norman and Uri Hasson. I did my undergrad in Mathematics and Psychology at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I originally came from Shanghai, China.
